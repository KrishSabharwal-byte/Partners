Public Policy

we recognise that the most effective policy interventions must balance sector-specific objectives with the country’s broader developmental goals. Our approach is built on the principle that law and regulation should not only resolve immediate concerns but also create enduring structures that support national priorities. We do not merely adapt to policy shifts—we actively contribute to shaping them. Through continuous engagement with government stakeholders and constructive participation in public discourse, we bring a principled, pragmatic, and legally sound perspective that prioritises impact, inclusivity, and systemic improvement.

Key Services

  • Legislative Drafting and Policy Formulation

    • We advise central and state governments, statutory authorities, and industry associations in conceptualising, drafting, and refining legislative instruments, regulatory frameworks, and public-private partnership models. Our contribution goes beyond drafting, offering strategic insights informed by extensive transactional expertise and sectoral knowledge.

  • Analytical Commentary and Consultation

    • We provide rigorous analysis of proposed laws and policy frameworks, assessing their implications for businesses and industries. Our commentary is frequently shared with clients, industry associations, and civil society stakeholders, and is also submitted in formal consultations with governmental bodies.

  • Policy Review and Reform

    • Where existing policies reveal inequities, inefficiencies, or legal infirmities, we identify reform opportunities and design actionable pathways. We also pursue principled policy reform through public interest litigation, blending advocacy with systemic enhancement.

Our Distinctive Approach

  • Integrated Legal Expertise

    • Our policy advisory is not siloed but cuts across multiple practice areas, drawing upon the collective insights of senior practitioners across the firm. This holistic approach ensures sharper, more effective outcomes.

  • Sector-Specific Insight

    • Our lawyers combine deep industry knowledge with decades of experience in high-value transactions and disputes, enabling us to deliver solutions that are both practical and principled.

  • Client-Centric Strategy

    • We begin each engagement by understanding client-specific challenges in the context of the wider legal and policy environment. This ensures that our advice is pragmatic, tailored, and commercially viable.

  • Strategic Policy Assessment

    • Grounded in sound legal principles, we evaluate gaps and inefficiencies in existing frameworks, providing workable recommendations that enhance clarity, compliance, and regulatory effectiveness.

  • Stakeholder Mapping and Engagement

    • We conduct comprehensive mapping of relevant actors—government agencies, regulators, financial institutions, and industry stakeholders—thereby enabling effective advocacy and informed decision-making.

Engagement with Government and Institutions

Our Partners maintain strong and trusted relationships with policymakers, regulators, and institutional actors, allowing us to engage credibly in principle-based policy dialogue. These relationships are founded on transparency, mutual respect, and professional integrity, ensuring our contributions are both respected and impactful.

Trusted by a Diverse Client Base

We advise a wide spectrum of stakeholders, including multinational and domestic corporations, industry associations, government agencies, and non-profit organisations. This diversity equips us with a multidimensional understanding of how policies impact various constituencies, enabling us to recommend reforms that are inclusive, balanced, and sustainable.
