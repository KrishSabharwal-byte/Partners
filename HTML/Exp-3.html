Artwork Advisory Practice

Our Artwork Advisory & Art Law Practice offers tailored legal solutions for the art world — from acquisitions and investments to cultural property, digital art, and disputes. We bridge law, commerce, and creativity to protect and enhance the value of art assets in India and internationally.

Key Services

  • Art Transactions & Structuring
    • Drafting, reviewing, and negotiating purchase, sale, and consignment agreements.
    • Advisory on cross-border acquisition and transfer of artworks.
    • Structuring art investments, art funds, and fractional ownership models.
  • Intellectual Property & Moral Rights
    • Protection and enforcement of copyrights, moral rights, and neighboring rights of artists.
    • Advisory on licensing, reproduction, digital display, and NFT/tokenization of art.
    • Resolving disputes relating to IP infringement and unauthorized use of artwork.
  • Provenance, Authentication & Due Diligence
    • Conducting legal due diligence on title, provenance, and authenticity of artworks.
    • Advisory on art valuation and risk assessment in acquisitions.
    • Ensuring compliance with international conventions on cultural property.
  • Cultural Property & Heritage Law
    • Advisory on repatriation, export, and import restrictions under Indian and international law.
    • Guidance on compliance with the Antiquities and Art Treasures Act, 1972 and UNESCO conventions.
    • Assisting museums, governments, and private collectors in heritage preservation.
  • Art Finance & Wealth Management
    • Structuring art-backed loans, collateralization, and insurance solutions.
    • Advisory on estate planning, trusts, and succession involving art collections.
    • Tax planning and cross-border structuring of art assets.
  • Digital & Emerging Art Forms
    • Legal support for digital art, NFTs, metaverse exhibitions, and blockchain-based provenance solutions.
    • Advisory on copyright, licensing, and ownership in digital collectibles.
    • Structuring platforms for trading digital and physical art assets.
  • Dispute Resolution
    • Representation in disputes relating to art transactions, authenticity, and ownership claims.
    • Arbitration and mediation for international art market disputes.
    • Cross-border litigation involving stolen or looted artworks.
