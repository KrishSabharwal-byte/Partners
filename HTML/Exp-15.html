Energy, Infrastructure & Projects

Our Projects Law Practice advises sponsors, lenders, investors, contractors, and government agencies on the development, financing, and operation of large-scale infrastructure and industrial projects. With expertise across energy, transport, urban infrastructure, and natural resources, we deliver comprehensive legal solutions that balance commercial objectives with regulatory compliance in India and globally.

Key Services

  • Project Development & Structuring
    • Advisory on project structuring, risk allocation, and contractual frameworks.
    • Drafting and negotiating concession agreements, EPC contracts, O&M agreements, and PPP frameworks.
    • Legal support across project lifecycles — from feasibility to commissioning and operation.
  • Project Finance & Investments
    • Structuring debt and equity financing for infrastructure and industrial projects.
    • Advisory on syndicated lending, export credit, multilateral financing, and green bonds.
    • Legal support for foreign direct investment (FDI) and joint ventures in Indian projects.
  • Energy & Natural Resources
    • Advisory on renewable energy (solar, wind, hydro, hydrogen, and waste-to-energy) and conventional energy projects.
    • Regulatory compliance for oil, gas, mining, and natural resource projects.
    • Structuring power purchase agreements (PPAs), fuel supply contracts, and energy trading arrangements.
  • Philanthropy & Social Impact
    • Advisory on setting up charitable trusts, Section 8 companies, and foundations.
    • Structuring and governance of philanthropic initiatives in India and internationally.
    • Ensuring compliance with FCRA and foreign donation regulations.
  • Infrastructure & Transport
    • Legal advisory for highways, ports, airports, metro rail, and logistics projects.
    • Structuring PPP and BOT models in transport infrastructure.
    • Negotiating concession agreements with government and regulatory bodies.
  • Urban Infrastructure & Smart Cities
    • Legal advisory on housing, water, sanitation, waste management, and urban renewal projects.
    • Structuring sustainable financing and public-private models for smart cities.
    • ESG-aligned advisory for urban and climate-resilient infrastructure.
  • Regulatory & Compliance Advisory
    • Assistance with project approvals, clearances, and licenses under Indian law.
    • Environmental and land acquisition compliance for large-scale projects.
    • Advisory on sector-specific regulations (electricity, telecom, oil & gas, transport).
  • Dispute Resolution
    • Representation in disputes arising from EPC contracts, concession agreements, and project finance.
    • Arbitration, mediation, and litigation support in infrastructure and energy-related conflicts.
    • Cross-border disputes involving international contractors, lenders, and investors.
