Asset Management & Funds Law Practice

Our Asset Management & Funds Law Practice advises fund managers, institutional investors, financial institutions, and high-net-worth individuals on structuring, regulatory compliance, and governance across the investment lifecycle. With deep knowledge of Indian securities regulations and strong international experience, we provide end-to-end legal support for private equity, venture capital, hedge funds, mutual funds, and alternative investment platforms.

Key Services

  • Art Transactions & Structuring
    • Drafting, reviewing, and negotiating purchase, sale, and consignment agreements.
    • Advisory on cross-border acquisition and transfer of artworks.
    • Structuring art investments, art funds, and fractional ownership models.
  • Fund Formation & Structuring
    • Establishing Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs), private equity, venture capital, real estate, hedge, and infrastructure funds in India.
    • Structuring domestic and offshore fund vehicles (LLPs, trusts, companies, and cross-border entities).
    • Drafting and negotiating fund documents, including Limited Partnership Agreements, Private Placement Memoranda, and Investment Management Agreements.
  • Regulatory & Compliance Advisory
    • Advisory on compliance with SEBI regulations, RBI guidelines, FEMA, and taxation laws.
    • Registration of AIFs, Portfolio Managers, and Foreign Venture Capital Investors (FVCIs).
    • Ongoing compliance, governance, and reporting obligations for fund managers and trustees.
  • Investments & Transactions
    • Legal support for fund investments in portfolio companies across sectors.
    • Structuring co-investments, side letters, and secondary transactions.
    • Negotiating exits, IPOs, trade sales, and other liquidity events.
  • Cross-Border Funds & Global Investors
    • Structuring offshore funds in Singapore, Mauritius, Luxembourg, Cayman Islands, and other jurisdictions.
    • Advisory on inbound and outbound investments, tax efficiency, and double taxation treaties.
    • Acting as India counsel for global asset managers and institutional investors.
  • Wealth & Asset Management
    • Advisory for family offices, HNIs, and institutional clients on investment structures and vehicles.
    • Structuring trusts, private client vehicles, and succession planning for fund investments.
    • Legal guidance on ESG and impact investment structures.
  • Dispute Resolution & Enforcement
    • Representation in investor disputes, fund governance issues, and exit-related conflicts.
    • Handling regulatory investigations and enforcement proceedings by SEBI, RBI, or tax authorities.
    • Arbitration and litigation involving fund managers, investors, or portfolio companies.
