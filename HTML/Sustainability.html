Commitment to Sustainability

At Kansal & Partners, sustainability is not just a corporate value—it is a responsibility that guides our legal practice, client advisory, and institutional culture. As India and the global community transition toward more sustainable models of growth, we recognise the critical role that law firms must play in shaping frameworks that balance economic progress, environmental stewardship, and social equity.

Our Perspective

Sustainability today transcends environmental concerns; it has become a core legal, regulatory, and governance issue. From climate-related disclosures and ESG compliance to green financing, renewable energy, and circular economy models, businesses are increasingly accountable not only to shareholders but also to regulators, communities, and future generations.

Our firm works at the intersection of these evolving obligations—helping clients navigate complex legal requirements, mitigate risks, and unlock opportunities in the sustainability domain.

Our Services in Sustainability and ESG

  • Advisory on ESG Regulations and Compliance: Guiding corporations on domestic and international ESG norms, sustainability reporting standards, and regulatory disclosures.
  • Climate Change and Environmental Law: Advising on renewable energy projects, carbon markets, emissions trading, and environmental impact assessments.
  • Sustainable Finance: Structuring and advising on green bonds, sustainability-linked loans, and financing mechanisms that promote low-carbon growth.
  • Corporate Governance: Embedding sustainability into governance frameworks, board responsibilities, and stakeholder engagement.
  • Policy and Advocacy: Engaging with regulators, industry associations, and government bodies on the development of forward-looking sustainability laws and frameworks.

Our Approach

      Principled and Pragmatic : We combine a strong foundation in legal principles with practical strategies tailored to each client’s business and sector.
      Cross-Sector Expertise : Our work spans industries such as energy, infrastructure, technology, manufacturing, aviation, and financial services—where sustainability considerations are rapidly evolving.
      Global Outlook with Local Insight : We advise international clients investing in India as well as domestic corporations with global aspirations, ensuring compliance with both Indian and international sustainability frameworks.
      Long-Term Partnership : Our aim is not limited to compliance; we help clients embed sustainability into the heart of their strategy, driving resilience and competitive advantage.

Our Commitment Within the Firm

We also take active steps to ensure that our own operations reflect our values. From reducing our environmental footprint and adopting responsible procurement practices to supporting pro bono initiatives in environmental justice and community development, sustainability remains integral to how we work.

Looking Ahead

As the world moves toward achieving the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and India advances its commitments under the Paris Agreement and its own net zero goals by 2070, we see sustainability not as an option but as an imperative. At Kansal & Partners, we are committed to partnering with clients, regulators, and communities in building a more sustainable, equitable, and resilient future.
