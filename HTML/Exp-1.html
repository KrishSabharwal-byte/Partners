Anti-Trust and Competition Law Practice

Our Antitrust and Competition Law Practice is a core pillar of the firm’s global offerings, combining deep knowledge of Indian competition law with international experience. We advise and represent clients across industries on the full spectrum of antitrust issues, from preventive compliance to complex litigation. With a strong presence in India and seamless coordination with our international offices and partner firms, we provide clients with pragmatic, strategic, and jurisdiction-specific solutions.

Key Services

  • Advisory & Compliance
    • Comprehensive legal advisory under the Competition Act, 2002 and allied regulations.
    • Structuring business arrangements, joint ventures, and collaborations to ensure antitrust compliance.
    • Drafting and implementing antitrust compliance programs tailored to clients’ business models.
    • Conducting competition audits, board-level briefings, and employee training.
  • Merger Control & Combinations
    • End-to-end legal support for merger filings before the Competition Commission of India (CCI).
    • Managing cross-border merger notifications in collaboration with leading global law firms.
    • Strategic structuring of deals to address competition concerns and accelerate approvals.
  • Investigations & Enforcement
    • Representation in CCI investigations relating to cartels, abuse of dominance, and bid-rigging.
    • Legal defense during dawn raids and regulatory proceedings.
    • Assistance with leniency applications and negotiation of settlement strategies.
    • Advising on overlaps between sectoral regulators and competition enforcement.
  • Litigation & Dispute Resolution
    • Advocacy before the CCI, National Company Law Appellate Tribunal (NCLAT), High Courts, and the Supreme Court of India.
    • Handling appeals, judicial reviews, and challenges to CCI orders.
    • Representing clients in antitrust damages claims and cross-border litigation.
  • Digital Economy & Emerging Markets
    • Legal guidance on competition issues in digital platforms, fintech, e-commerce, and tech-driven ecosystems.
    • Advising on market dominance, data-sharing, and algorithmic pricing risks.
    • Developing forward-looking compliance strategies for AI and new economy businesses.
  • Cross-Border & Global Practice
    • Supporting Indian corporations on compliance with EU, US, and Asian antitrust frameworks.
    • Acting as local counsel for international firms on India-specific filings and investigations.
    • Coordinating multi-jurisdictional defense strategies in global cartel probes and merger reviews.
