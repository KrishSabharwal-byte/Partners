Tax

India’s complex and evolving tax regime demands precise navigation, strategic planning, and compliance expertise. As a fast-growing economy attracting significant global investment, India presents unique opportunities—but also multifaceted tax challenges for domestic and cross-border transactions.

Our Tax Law Practice offers end-to-end advisory, litigation, and compliance services, catering to multinational corporations, high-net-worth individuals, start-ups, and institutional investors operating in or with India.

Tax

  • We provide comprehensive legal support across the full spectrum of direct and indirect taxes, including:
    • Corporate Tax – Advisory on domestic taxation, transfer pricing, and tax structuring for Indian subsidiaries and joint ventures.
    • International Taxation – Cross-border tax planning, Double Taxation Avoidance Agreements (DTAA) interpretation, Base Erosion and Profit Shifting (BEPS) compliance, and global tax restructuring.
    • Goods & Services Tax (GST) – Structuring supply chains, GST registration and compliance, dispute resolution, and advisory on classification, valuation, and place of supply.
    • Withholding Tax & TDS – Advisory on deduction, remittance, and refund procedures under Indian law.
    • Tax Litigation & Dispute Resolution – Representation before income tax authorities, appellate tribunals, High Courts, and the Supreme Court of India.
    • Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A) Tax Advisory – Tax-efficient structuring of mergers, demergers, and acquisitions, including due diligence and post-transaction integration.
    • Tax Policy & Regulatory Compliance – Navigating evolving tax codes, including the Income Tax Bill, 2025, GST amendments, and sector-specific incentives.
