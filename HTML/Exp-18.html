Restructuring, Special Situations & Insolvency

Our Restructuring, Special Situations & Insolvency Law Practice advises creditors, debtors, investors, and resolution professionals on navigating financial distress, turnaround strategies, and insolvency proceedings. With deep expertise in the Indian Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC), distressed asset transactions, and cross-border restructurings, we deliver practical, commercially driven solutions to preserve value and resolve complex situations.

Key Services

  • Corporate Restructuring & Turnaround
    • Advisory on debt restructuring, refinancing, and distressed M&A.
    • Negotiating standstill agreements, debt-to-equity swaps, and settlement structures.
    • Structuring schemes of arrangement and compromise under the Companies Act.
  • Insolvency & Bankruptcy (IBC) Advisory
    • Representation of creditors, debtors, and resolution professionals before the NCLT, NCLAT, High Courts, and the Supreme Court of India.
    • Drafting and submission of resolution plans, including acquisition of distressed companies.
    • Advisory on liquidation, voluntary insolvency, and individual insolvency proceedings.
  • Distressed Asset Transactions
    • Structuring acquisitions of stressed assets through Insolvency Resolution Process, SARFAESI, and DRT mechanisms.
    • Advisory on participation in distressed debt auctions and asset reconstruction companies (ARCs).
    • Legal support for private equity and special situation funds investing in distressed opportunities.
  • Cross-Border Insolvency & Special Situations
    • Guidance on cross-border insolvency, recognition, and enforcement of foreign proceedings.
    • Advisory on restructuring of multinational corporate groups with Indian exposure.
    • Coordination with international counsel in multi-jurisdictional insolvency and restructuring matters.
  • Banking & Creditor Rights
    • Representation of banks, financial institutions, and bondholders in recovery proceedings.
    • Legal support in enforcement actions under SARFAESI Act and Debt Recovery Tribunal (DRT).
    • Structuring inter-creditor agreements and enforcement strategies.
  • Dispute Resolution
    • Litigation support in insolvency-related disputes, avoidance transactions, and fraudulent preference claims.
    • Arbitration and mediation for debt restructuring and settlement disputes.
    • Representation in shareholder and creditor disputes in special situation transactions.
