Environment and Climate Change Law Practice

Our Environment & Climate Change Law Practice provides comprehensive legal and regulatory support to businesses, investors, and governments on sustainability, environmental compliance, and climate-related risks. We advise clients across industries on aligning their operations with domestic environmental laws and international climate frameworks, helping them achieve sustainable growth while mitigating legal and reputational risks.

Key Services

  • Regulatory & Compliance Advisory
    • Advisory on compliance with the Environment Protection Act, Air Act, Water Act, and related environmental regulations.
    • Guidance on waste management, hazardous substances, and extended producer responsibility (EPR).
    • Environmental clearance and approvals for infrastructure, energy, and industrial projects.
  • Climate Change & Sustainability
    • Advisory on India’s climate policies, NDC commitments, and renewable energy regulations.
    • Structuring climate finance and carbon credit transactions.
    • Legal support for ESG integration, green financing, and sustainable supply chain initiatives.
  • Energy & Natural Resources
    • Regulatory support in renewable energy, clean technology, and sustainable infrastructure projects.
    • Legal advisory on mining, water use, and natural resource management.
    • Structuring public-private partnerships in sustainable development projects.
  • Cross-Border & International Frameworks
    • Advisory on compliance with global frameworks, including the Paris Agreement, UNFCCC, and EU sustainability directives.
    • Structuring cross-border investments in renewable energy and climate tech.
    • Supporting multinational corporations in aligning Indian operations with global ESG standards.
  • Litigation & Dispute Resolution
    • Representation in environmental litigation before the National Green Tribunal (NGT), High Courts, and the Supreme Court of India.
    • Handling disputes relating to environmental clearances, pollution liability, and climate damages claims.
    • Advising on arbitration and disputes in carbon trading and renewable energy contracts.
  • Corporate Governance & Risk Management
    • Drafting and implementing environmental and climate governance policies.
    • Advisory on disclosure obligations relating to sustainability and ESG metrics.
    • Risk assessments for climate change impacts on business operations and investments.
