Labour & Employment Law Practice

Our Labour & Employment Law Practice provides strategic legal counsel to employers, multinational corporations, and employees on the full spectrum of employment law issues in India and across borders. We help clients navigate the complexities of India’s labour framework, ensure compliance with evolving regulations, and manage workforce challenges in a dynamic business environment.

Key Services

  • Employment Advisory & Compliance
    • Advisory on compliance with the Indian Labour Codes, state-specific labour laws, and sectoral regulations.
    • Drafting and reviewing employment contracts, HR policies, employee handbooks, and codes of conduct.
    • Advisory on employee benefits, wage structures, gratuity, provident fund, and social security laws.
  • Workforce Management & Restructuring
    • Legal support in workforce restructuring, downsizing, redundancy, and voluntary retirement schemes.
    • Advisory on transfer of employees in mergers, acquisitions, and business reorganisations.
    • Structuring employee stock option plans (ESOPs), incentive schemes, and executive compensation.
  • Dispute Resolution & Litigation
    • Representation before labour courts, industrial tribunals, High Courts, and the Supreme Court of India.
    • Handling disputes relating to wrongful termination, unfair labour practices, and employment benefits.
    • Advisory and representation in trade union negotiations, strikes, and industrial disputes.
  • Investigations & Workplace Policies
    • Advisory on prevention of sexual harassment (POSH) compliance and internal committee constitution.
    • Conducting workplace investigations into misconduct, fraud, harassment, and whistleblower complaints.
    • Designing grievance redressal mechanisms and internal compliance frameworks.
  • Health, Safety & Workplace Regulations
    • Advisory on occupational health and safety standards under Indian law.
    • Compliance strategies for manufacturing, construction, and hazardous industries.
    • Guidance on remote work policies, workplace digitisation, and gig economy regulations.
  • Cross-Border Employment & Global Mobility
    • Advisory on secondments, expatriate employment, and cross-border workforce mobility.
    • Structuring global employment contracts in alignment with Indian law.
    • Coordinating with international law firms on multi-jurisdictional employment matters.
