Artificial Intelligence (AI) Law Practice

Our Artificial Intelligence (AI) Law Practice is at the forefront of advising clients on the legal, regulatory, and ethical dimensions of AI adoption and innovation. We work with technology companies, startups, enterprises, investors, and governments to help them harness AI responsibly while ensuring compliance with evolving domestic and global regulatory landscapes.

Key Services

  • Regulatory & Compliance Advisory
    • Guidance on compliance with India’s evolving AI, data protection, and digital economy laws.
    • Monitoring and advising on global AI regulatory developments, including the EU AI Act, U.S. frameworks, and OECD/UN guidelines.
    • Structuring AI governance frameworks and ethical use policies for enterprises.
  • Contracts & Commercial Transactions
    • Drafting and negotiating AI development, licensing, and deployment agreements.
    • Structuring data-sharing and cloud-computing contracts aligned with IP, privacy, and antitrust requirements.
    • Advising on joint ventures, collaborations, and partnerships in AI-driven projects.
  • Intellectual Property (IP) in AI
    • Advisory on ownership of AI-generated works and protection of training data.
    • Structuring IP strategies for algorithms, software, and AI solutions.
    • Managing licensing, commercialization, and disputes around AI-related IP.
  • Liability & Risk Management
    • Advising on liability frameworks for autonomous systems, algorithmic decision-making, and AI-driven products.
    • Designing risk allocation clauses in contracts to mitigate exposure.
    • Legal due diligence for AI products, including bias, transparency, and explainability concerns.
  • Data, Privacy & Cybersecurity
    • Compliance with India’s Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and global data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.).
    • Advisory on cross-border data transfers, storage, and cloud governance.
    • Guidance on data security, cybersecurity audits, and breach response protocols.
  • Sector-Specific AI Applications
    • Legal advisory for AI adoption in finance, healthcare, manufacturing, autonomous mobility, e-commerce, and defense.
    • Addressing industry-specific regulatory overlap (e.g., RBI for AI in fintech, CDSCO for AI in medical devices).
    • Risk assessment and regulatory clearance for high-risk AI systems.
  • Dispute Resolution & Litigation
    • Representation in disputes relating to AI-related contrategic counsel in cross-border litigation involving AI technologies.
    • Support in arbitration and mediation of AI-driven commercial conflicts.
